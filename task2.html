A food restaurant is a dining establishment where people can enjoy meals prepared by professional chefs. It offers a variety of cuisines and dishes, catering to diverse tastes and dietary preferences. The ambiance and service quality significantly enhance the dining experience. Many restaurants focus on using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. They often serve as social hubs for gatherings, celebrations, and casual outings.

chicken Briyani

Chicken biryani is a popular South Asian dish that combines fragrant basmati rice with marinated chicken, spices, and herbs. Traditionally cooked in a layered method, it often includes ingredients like saffron, yogurt, and fried onions

Burger

burger is a popular sandwich consisting of a cooked patty, typically beef, placed inside a sliced bun. It often includes toppings such as lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, cheese, and condiments like ketchup and mustard.

French Fries

French fries, often simply called fries, are deep-fried potato strips typically served as a side dish. Originating in Belgium, they became a popular snack and fast food item worldwide.
